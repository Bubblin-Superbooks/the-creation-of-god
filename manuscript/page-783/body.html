Chapter XXV.

The Ecclesiastical Kindergarten.

What shall we do to be saved? is a question asked by every religious fanatic.

Saved from what? Ignorance? Superstition? Bigotry? Or stupidity? From idiocy or imbecility? Or, are we to be saved from poverty, hunger, starvation, misery and wretchedness, distress and degradation?
