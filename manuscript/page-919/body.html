Chapter XXX.

The Non Credo.

Religion, supernaturalism, ecclesiastical control of human affairs, have done more harm than the good they have ever effected. For several thousand years they have been doing the worst of mischief—in spite of their conceited belief to the contrary—to actual enlightenment, to the advancement and prosperity of the masses, to the progress of nations generally.
